Hello. My name is Rhian.
I have always believed in the power of the visual arts. Whether it’s through technology or performance, I am in love with the creation and process of designing an aesthetic experience for others. It’s like seasoning life with a little more charm.
So I can design some things for you. I can also film some things for you. But overall, I’m here to bring visual beauty to your vision.

VIDEOGRAPHY

Film is my favorite medium. Put a camera in my hands and we’re good to go. I have had professional experience in both filmmaking and event videography and can honestly say I don’t have a preference. The beauty within filmmaking is the notion of taking a story – fact or fiction – and turning it into something visually real. Like an uncharted world we get to jump into. And the beauty in event videography is just the idea that you can capture a single moment and keep it forever. Like a stolen moment in time. Both, such beautiful concepts. But out of the many integral departments on set, video village is where I feel the most at home. Most of the positions I’ve held on crews were either as editor or assistant editor. Because of so, I have experience editing narratives, documentaries, stop-motion, and even animation.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

My experience in graphic design can go as far back as 2008 in the Toby Johnson Middle School Yearbook classroom. From color palettes to font choices, I have been blessed with the opportunity to learn basic graphic designing throughout all my years in academia, professionally learning the ropes of this field in University. Graphic designing has been the most seamless transition from hobby to career. Creating something out of nothing – the paintbrush as my mouse and keyboard – is one of the most soothing and satisfying feelings. Well… okay, maybe it’s not always soothing when Adobe Illustrator decides to crash on me five times in a row. But with over ten years of experience in graphic designing, the visual beauty that comes from a well designed and thought out piece will remain one of my many joys.

WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

Yes, I am a devoted designer and videographer, but everyone who knows me personally will never deny that my cultural dancing is probably one of my most defining characteristics. My culture has my entire heart. I am a first generation Pilipinx-American, with my mother’s family in Manila and my father’s family in Kalibo. From a young age, I was raised with the morals of the Philippines, but being born in California, I lived an American childhood. I was thirteen when I found Tinikling – the national dance of the Philippines which comprises of dancing in between two bamboo sticks rhythmically – and I can truly say my life changed that day. Since then, I have been devoted to performing all the beautiful dances from the Philippines. The narrative of the Pilipinx-American, of holding onto our culture overseas, is such a unique and untold narrative that deserves to be heard. To my fellow Kababayan, Isang Bagsak.
